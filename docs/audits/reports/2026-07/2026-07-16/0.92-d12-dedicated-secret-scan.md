## Fail-Closed Behavior

- Missing or non-executable scanner paths reject before scanning.
- A scanner that cannot report its version rejects with a distinct cause.
- A reported version different from 8.30.1 rejects before scanning.
- A scanner exit with one or more redacted findings reports only the candidate
  count and deletes the temporary report.
- A scanner exit without a parseable redacted finding report remains an
  operational failure rather than being reported as a clean result.
- The integrity guard requires the installer, version/checksum pins, CI scan,
  full redaction flag, exact fingerprint file, and valid fingerprint syntax.
