## Verdict

0.95 is closed at `v0.95.10`. The line has one timer scheduler, one
direct IC timer boundary, six built-in owner identities, and no surviving
fixed polling flow for empty event/deadline work. All thirteen admitted
findings are fixed. No P0, open P1/P2, compatibility path, or blocked owner

child could request cycles before its parent completed its own grant in the
same timer round. Insufficient parent capacity was also flattened at the RPC

## Recovery And Failure Bounds

- Transport and ops failures, plus an in-flight conflict, use the frozen
  1/2/4/8/16/30-minute progression.
- A typed `ResourceExhausted` funding response receives one one-minute recovery
  attempt between successful grants. A second exhaustion stops failed.
- Authorization, configuration, local invariant, arithmetic overflow, and
  malformed response failures stop failed with their typed cause.
- Parent direct-child admission, replay exclusion, cost guard, request clamp,
  cumulative budget, cooldown, kill switch, and available balance remain
  authoritative. The child cannot cause automatic root ICP conversion.
- Lifecycle rebuild uses authoritative stable work and derived indexes. Empty
  or disabled owners remain unregistered rather than polling for work.
