## Hard-Cut Contract

- New application operations carry one required absolute replay deadline.
- Exact retained retries remain observable after their deadline; an absent
  operation at or after the deadline rejects before mutation or external work.
- The maximum remaining replay window is one hour, inclusive. Consumers may
  use shorter immutable domain deadlines.
- Terminal observation grace is 15 minutes. Work resolved no later than its
  replay deadline is reclaimable within 75 minutes of admission at the maximum
  window; later authoritative recovery receives 15 minutes after settlement.
- Reclamation removes only receipt history and its exact adjuncts. Committed
  resource totals remain authoritative; rolled-back zero totals are absent.
- No optional deadline, default, old-limit reader, 24-hour mode, alias,
  fallback, compatibility shim, or public removal endpoint remains.
